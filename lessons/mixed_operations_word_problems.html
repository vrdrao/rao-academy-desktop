Ananya worked out 403 − 158 and got 355. The correct answer is 245. What did she do wrong?

  • She added the two numbers instead of subtracting.
  • She never regrouped — in each column she just took the smaller digit from the bigger one.
  • She subtracted 158 from 400 and forgot the 3.
  • She lined up the digits in the wrong columns.

In the tens column she needed 0 − 5, which is impossible, so she flipped it to 5 − 0 = 5. She should have regrouped instead, which gives 9 − 5 = 4. That single 5 in place of a 4 is why she got 355 instead of 245.
