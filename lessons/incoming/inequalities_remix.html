Which inequality does this number line show?

  • greater than 2
  • less than 3
  • less than or equal to 1

Filled dot on 1 + arrow left = 1 or less: x ≤ 1. The filled dot means 1 is included.

Tricky one! Which number line shows numbers that are LESS THAN 3? (not equal to 3)

'Less than 3' does NOT include 3, so the dot must be OPEN (hollow). The filled dot would mean ≤ 3.

Tap the sign that makes it true: x ? 15

The dot is OPEN on 15 and shaded right, so x is greater than 15 (not equal): x > 15.
