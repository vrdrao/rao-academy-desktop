## SPEC AMENDMENT — this brief changes ratified law, deliberately

CALM CARD LAW 3 (Brief 7.6) previously read: "A wrong attempt marks the tried
option with a small red ✕ … The ✕ persists for the life of the question."

LAW 3 IS AMENDED TO:

  WRONG IS A WHISPER, AND THE WHISPER DOES NOT LINGER. A wrong attempt
  produces no persistent mark on the task. When the child taps Try Again,
  the task returns to EXACTLY its first-attempt state: no ✕, no residual
  selection, no retained input, no moved tiles. The child re-reads the
  question cold.

Venkat's rationale, recorded: a child who just answered knows what they
picked; the ✕ added nothing. On multi-select it actively lied — a red ✕ on
"2" in "select all the even numbers" teaches that 2 is odd.

LAW 4 (HELP ACCUMULATES) IS UNCHANGED AND MUST NOT BE WEAKENED BY THIS WORK.
Hint bubbles and walkthrough steps persist exactly as today. Help is not
residue. The chat log stays; the TASK resets. If any change you make causes
a hint bubble to disappear, clear, or re-render on Try Again, you have
broken law 4 — stop and report.

Update `docs/CALM-CARD-MASTER-SPEC-v1.md` law 3 to the amended text, with a
dated line noting it supersedes the Brief 7.6 wording. Do not edit the
historical Brief 7.6 text in BRIEFS.md — it is a record of what was ratified
then.

## PHASE 2 — the fix (only after Venkat's go)

Design constraint: ONE reset mechanism, not eleven special cases. Prefer
snapshotting first-attempt state at question mount and restoring from the
snapshot, over per-behavior teardown logic — a snapshot cannot drift out of
sync with a behavior's rendering the way hand-written teardown does. If the
DIAG shows snapshot-and-restore is not viable for some behavior, say which
and why, and propose the alternative for that behavior only.

Requirements:

1. Try Again restores first-attempt task state for every behavior in the
   phase-1 table. No exceptions, no "deferred to a later brief" entries
   unless Venkat rules one.
2. `markTried()` and the `.cc-x` DOM insertion are removed. Leave the `.cc-x`
   CSS rule in `engine/rao-card.css` in place but commented as retired with
   this brief's name and date — do not delete it in the same commit that
   changes behavior.
3. Hint bubbles and walkthrough steps are untouched by the reset.
4. Attempt counting, `walkOffered()`, outcome recording, and the
   lock-on-walkthrough-open behavior are ALL unchanged. The reset is visual
   and input state only. It must not reset the child's progress toward the
   "Walk me through it" offer.
5. Law 2 holds throughout: no green anywhere while the question is
   attemptable.
